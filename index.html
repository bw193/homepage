About Our Factory

A Legacy of Quality Since 2005

Founded in 2005, Jiaxing Chengtai Mirror Co., Ltd. is a professional R&D and manufacturing enterprise for LED mirrors, bathroom mirrors, and full-length mirrors. We operate a 25,000 sqm self-built factory in Jiaxing, Zhejiang, just 60km from Shanghai Port.

We own registered brands BOLEN (EU/US), Niccy, and Thiamo. As a National High-tech Enterprise, we hold 65+ patents and operate a municipal-level R&D center. Our products are certified by CE, SAA, UL, CCC, IP44/IP66, RoHS, and ISO9001.
